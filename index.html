The book I read is called Ranger's Apprentice and it is awesome.

Here is some interesting information about it.

  • The full title "The Sorcerer Of The North".
  • The AR book level is 6.1!
  • The author is none other than John Flanagan author of so many great books!
  • The book rating is 4.2 stars!
  • The main character is Will who is a ranger sent in disguise to investigate rumors of a sorcerer in the north.

This book is great for anyone who likes cliffhangers. The Plot really gets started off when Will is called from his station at the borders of the kingdom to a secret ranger meeting where he is told to travel to the northern most reaches of the kingdom to investigate rumors of a sorcerer!

But will he be able to when he sees a mysterious black night standing in the woods scaring away visitors for an unknown reason, when the son of the lord falls ill, and when his childhood friend is kidnapped by a greedy knight in search of power?

Here are some reasons why you should read it.

  • The book is thrilling and fast paced with an excellent plot.
  • It has a variety of themes and interesting roles for the characters.
  • The plot flows so smoothly that many a reader will find themselves up past their bedtime.
  • This book has a wonderful sense of mystery and suspense while maintaining a fast action filled plot.
